# Deleting Keystore Entries



## Context

This option is not available for keystore entries owned by SAP.



## Procedure

1.  Choose the *Keystore* tile in the *Manage Security* section.

2.  On the *Current* tab, select a keystore entry.

3.  Choose the <span class="SAP-icons">ÓÅö</span> \(Actions\) icon, then select *Delete*. Alternatively, you can click the keystore entry alias to open the details, and then choose *Delete*.

    > ### Caution:  
    > You cannot delete SAP-owned keystore entries.
